How it works

Three dots. One connection.

Wanderly connects people around shared plans — not just shared tastes. Post your trip, get matched to real travelers heading the same way, and go at your own pace.

🗺️

Post your trip

Tell Wanderly where you're going, when, and the kind of experience you're after. A weekend in Bali. Three weeks through Vietnam. Any shape, any pace.

✈️

Connect the dots

Your destination, dates, travel style — and a compatibility score that shows exactly why you'd travel well together. Not guesswork. Real overlap.

🔄

Your terms, always

Chat, plan, and choose your level — one meal, a few days, the whole journey. No obligation. Change your mind anytime. It's your adventure.

What makes Wanderly different

Built around how people actually want to travel

🎯

Trip-first matching

Most apps match you to a person and hope for the best. Wanderly matches you around a real plan — destination, dates, vibe — and surfaces the people who genuinely fit it. Your compatibility score tells you why.

🔄

Your adventure, your terms

One coffee. A day hike. The whole trip. Set your own level and change it anytime — no obligation, ever.

Find your tribe

Launching across Southeast Asia's top travel destinations. The right companion for your next trip is already out there.

Plan together, in-app

Chat, share itineraries, and coordinate in one place. In-chat translation across 50+ languages so language is never a barrier.

Two-way reviews

After every trip, both travelers review each other. Your reputation grows with every connection — building a community you can trust.

🤝

Women-first mode

A dedicated women-only pool available to any female traveler — on by default or toggled anytime, no explanation needed.

Travel safer, together

Safety isn't a feature. It's the product.

Meeting a stranger should feel safe by design — not by luck. Wanderly builds trust into every step, before you say hello and long after you part ways.

  • 🪪
    Photo & liveness verification
    Every Wanderly profile goes through a photo and real-time liveness check. You always know who you're connecting with.
  • 📍
    Live location sharing
    Share your real-time location with up to 5 trusted contacts during any meet-up. Auto-expires when you're done.
  • 📋
    Pre-meet safety checklist
    A built-in guide before every first meeting — public venue suggestions, what to share with your people, and tips from verified travelers.
  • 🆘
    SOS — always one tap away
    Instantly alerts your trusted contacts and local emergency services, accessible from any screen at any time.

Who it's for

Every traveler has a different kind of tribe they're looking for

Wanderly is built for anyone who travels and wants genuine human connection along the way — whether that's a meal, a hike, or a whole adventure.

🥾

The adventure seeker

Looking for a hiking companion who can keep up and keep the conversation going — not just someone heading the same direction.

→ Treks, off-beat routes, shared risk
🍜

The experience hunter

City trips built around food, culture, and local life. Wants a companion for the experience — not necessarily the full trip.

→ Dinners, day trips, city exploration
💻

The long-term traveler

Moving cities every few weeks. Needs real connection in each new place — not just a co-working desk to sit at.

→ Community, regulars, local knowledge
🛳️

The seasoned explorer

Cultural tours and cruises. Wants peer-aged companions who share the same pace — and help split the costs along the way.

→ Comfort travel, cultural depth
🎒

The budget backpacker

Making the most of every dollar. Safety in numbers, new friendships, and someone to split a boat taxi with.

→ Cost-sharing, island hopping, hostels
🌸

The safety-conscious traveler

Wants to meet people and explore freely — but on her own terms, with the confidence that every connection is verified and safe.

→ Women-only mode, verified profiles
