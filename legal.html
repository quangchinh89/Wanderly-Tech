Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026  ·  Effective: 18 June 2026

Wanderly ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding your data. By using the App you agree to the collection and use of your data as described here.

1. Data We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Account information: name, email address, date of birth, gender, home country, profile photo.
  • Identity verification data: government ID image, selfie/liveness scan (processed by our third-party provider; raw ID images deleted within 30 days).
  • Profile content: bio, travel interests, travel style, languages, past trips, uploaded photos.
  • Trip data: destinations, dates, itineraries, group size preferences.
  • Communications: messages sent and received through the in-app chat.
  • Reviews and ratings you submit about other users.
  • Safety data: trusted contacts you designate, location data shared during SOS events (only when you activate the feature).

Data Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device type, OS version, app version, unique device identifiers.
  • Usage data: features used, screens viewed, interactions within the App, crash reports.
  • Approximate location: city-level location used for match recommendations (precise GPS only when you explicitly activate location sharing or SOS).
  • Log data: IP address, access timestamps, referring URLs.

Data from Third Parties

  • Social login providers (Google, Facebook): profile name, email, profile picture — only what you authorize.
  • Identity verification providers: pass/fail result and a verification reference ID (not the raw ID document).

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Match you with compatible travelers based on destination, dates, interests, and travel style.
  • Enable in-app communication between matched users.
  • Operate safety features: SOS alerts, live-location sharing, trusted contact notifications, and incident reporting.
  • Verify your identity to build community trust.
  • Send transactional notifications (match alerts, messages, trip updates, safety check-ins).
  • Improve the App through analytics, A/B testing, and crash diagnostics.
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: processing necessary to provide the App's core services.
  • Legitimate interests: safety enforcement, fraud prevention, improving our services.
  • Consent: marketing communications; precise location sharing; processing sensitive data (you may withdraw consent at any time).
  • Legal obligation: compliance with applicable law, court orders, or regulatory requirements.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only:

  • With other users: your public profile information, trip details, and reviews are visible to other Wanderly users as intended by the App's functionality.
  • With service providers: identity verification, cloud hosting, analytics, push notification, and customer support providers — each bound by data processing agreements.
  • With trusted contacts: location data and SOS alerts, only when you activate these features.
  • For legal reasons: if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Wanderly or others.
  • In a business transfer: if Wanderly is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred. We will notify you before your data is subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Identity Verification Data

Government ID images submitted for verification are processed exclusively by our third-party verification provider. Wanderly stores only the verification status (verified/unverified) and a reference token. Raw ID images are deleted within 30 days of submission. Selfie/liveness data is used solely for verification and is not used for facial recognition or marketing.

6. Location Data

We collect city-level approximate location to power match recommendations and trip discovery. We collect precise GPS location only when you explicitly activate live-location sharing or trigger an SOS event. You can disable location access at any time via your device settings, though this may limit match accuracy.

7. Data Retention

  • Active account data: retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted account data: most data deleted within 30 days of account deletion. Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or prevent fraud.
  • Chat messages: retained for 90 days after a trip ends, then deleted.
  • Government ID images: deleted within 30 days of submission (handled by verification provider).
  • Anonymised analytics data: may be retained indefinitely.

8. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security and encourage you to use strong, unique passwords.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Restriction: request that we limit our processing of your data.
  • Object: object to processing based on legitimate interests or for direct marketing.
  • Withdraw consent: at any time, where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@wanderly.tech. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Wanderly is not intended for anyone under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If we discover that a minor has registered, we will delete their account and data promptly. If you believe a minor is using the App, please contact us at support@wanderly.tech.

11. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than where you reside, including countries that may not have the same data protection laws. Where we transfer data internationally, we implement appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) as required by applicable law.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email at least 14 days before they take effect. Your continued use of the App after the effective date constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact

Data controller: Wanderly  ·  Email: wanderly@wanderly.tech

For GDPR-related enquiries or to lodge a complaint, you may also contact your local data protection authority.

Community Guidelines

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Wanderly is built on a simple promise: travel is better when it's safe, inclusive, and genuine. These Community Guidelines define the standards that protect our community. Violating them may result in warnings, content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent banning — depending on severity.

Our Core Values

🛡️ Safety firstEvery feature and decision prioritizes your physical and emotional safety.
🤝 Respect for allRegardless of gender, race, nationality, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or background.
✅ AuthenticityReal people, real plans, real connections.
✈️ Travel-only intentWanderly is a travel companion app. Dating, solicitation, or misuse undermines everyone's trust.

1. Be Who You Say You Are

Authenticity is the foundation of trust in our community.

  • Use your real name and genuine photos. No aliases, fake personas, or stock images.
  • Do not misrepresent your age, gender, location, or travel plans.
  • Do not create multiple accounts. If banned, do not re-register.
  • Do not impersonate another user, celebrity, or public figure.

2. Treat Every Traveler with Respect

Harassment and discrimination have no place in the Wanderly community.

Harassment — Strictly Prohibited

  • Sending unwanted sexual messages, images, or advances.
  • Continuing to contact someone after they have asked you to stop.
  • Threatening, intimidating, or bullying any user.
  • Doxxing — sharing another user's private information without their consent.
  • Posting or sharing content designed to humiliate, shame, or harm another person.

Discrimination — Zero Tolerance

  • Slurs, hate speech, or content that demeans people based on gender, race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, sexual orientation, gender identity, disability, or age.
  • Refusing to travel with someone on discriminatory grounds (Wanderly reserves the right to investigate such reports).

3. No Sexual or Explicit Content

Wanderly is a travel companion platform, not a dating or adult content app.

Immediate suspension: Violations of this section will result in immediate account suspension pending review.
  • Do not post nudity, sexually explicit content, or suggestive photos in your profile or in chat.
  • Do not solicit sexual services or arrange paid companionship of any kind.
  • Do not use Wanderly to seek or offer romantic or sexual relationships. Use a dating app.
  • Do not send unsolicited sexual messages, photos, or propositions.

4. No Illegal Activity

You must not use Wanderly to plan, facilitate, or promote any illegal activity, including but not limited to:

  • Drug trafficking, purchase, or distribution.
  • Human trafficking, smuggling, or exploitation of any kind.
  • Fraud, theft, money laundering, or financial scams.
  • Illegal border crossings or document forgery.
  • Any activity that violates local law in any destination.

We cooperate fully with law enforcement investigations. If you witness illegal activity, report it immediately using the in-app report function and contact local authorities.

5. Be Honest About Your Travel Plans

Wanderly matches are built around real trips. Misrepresenting your plans wastes others' time and undermines trust.

  • Only create trips you genuinely intend to take.
  • Update or cancel your trip if your plans change — do not leave accepted members without notice.
  • Do not create fictional trips to collect matches or inflate your profile statistics.

6. Protect Others' Privacy

  • Do not share another user's personal information (phone number, address, workplace, social media handles) with third parties without their explicit consent.
  • Do not record voice or video calls without all participants' consent, where required by law.
  • Do not screenshot or share private chat messages publicly.

7. Keep It Safe — Before You Meet

We strongly encourage all users to follow these safety practices before meeting anyone for the first time:

  • Video call before meeting in person.
  • Meet in a public place for your first meeting.
  • Tell a trusted contact where you are going and who you are meeting.
  • Use the in-app live-location sharing feature.
  • Trust your instincts — if something feels wrong, you are never obligated to continue.

Wanderly provides safety tools but cannot guarantee your safety. Your personal judgment and precautions are essential.

8. Use the App as Intended

  • Do not use Wanderly primarily as a social media platform, networking tool, or marketing channel.
  • Do not solicit investment, donations, or sales from other users.
  • Do not post referral links, affiliate links, or promotional codes without our written permission.
  • Do not attempt to move conversations off-platform in order to circumvent Wanderly's safety features.

9. Reviews Must Be Honest

  • Only leave reviews for trips you have genuinely completed with another user.
  • Be honest and fair. Do not leave retaliatory reviews.
  • Do not offer incentives or make requests for positive reviews.
  • False reviews — positive or negative — may be removed and may result in account action.

10. How to Report

If you encounter content or behaviour that violates these Guidelines:

  • Tap the (⋯) menu on any profile or message and select "Report."
  • Select the most accurate category and provide details.
  • Our Trust & Safety team reviews all reports and responds based on our best effort.

For emergencies, use the SOS feature and contact local emergency services directly. Wanderly's in-app reporting is not a substitute for emergency services.

11. Consequences

Violation of these Guidelines may result in:

  • Content removal without notice.
  • A formal warning and account restriction.
  • Temporary suspension.
  • Permanent ban with no right to re-register.
  • Reporting to law enforcement where required by law or where we believe there is risk of harm.

The severity of the response will reflect the severity of the violation. We do not issue warnings for the most serious violations (sexual exploitation, illegal activity, threats of violence).

12. Appeals

If you believe your account was actioned in error, you may submit an appeal at support@wanderly.tech within 14 days of the action. We will review all appeals fairly. Our decision on appeal is final.

Thank you for helping make Wanderly the safest travel community in the world.
Questions? Contact us at support@wanderly.tech
